Gyuhap Chongseo

The encyclopedia of women’s wisdom

In 1809, the remarkable Lady Bingheo (憑虛閣 李氏) completed what would become the most comprehensive guide to Korean women’s wisdom: the Gyuhap Chongseo (閨閤叢書).

“Within these pages lie the secrets of radiant skin achieved through natural harmony — the very principles that guide our formulations today.”

This extraordinary four-volume encyclopedia contained everything from royal beauty formulations to seasonal skincare rituals, preserving centuries of knowledge that might otherwise have been lost to time.

Cultural legacy

“Beauty in Korean culture has always been inseparable from virtue, health and harmony with nature. The Gyuhap Chongseo stands as testament to this philosophy — a sacred text that preserved not merely beauty formulations, but a profound way of living that honours both inner cultivation and outer expression.”

Living heritage

Today, we are the guardians of this extraordinary legacy. Every formulation honours the wisdom of Lady Bingheo; every ritual connects us to centuries of Korean women who understood that true beauty emanates from the harmony between natural ingredients and mindful practice.
